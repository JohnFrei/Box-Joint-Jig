Wood for the gears

Use 9 mm or 11 mm birch plywood. If you buy a sheet 2x4' or larger for the base, there should be enough material left for quite a few gears. For each gear, you can calculate the size of the minimum square blank by the following calculation:
    Gear size in centimeters = Number of teeth * 0.51 + 1
Its a good idea to make the blanks slightly larger than the final gear.

For the 12 tooth gear, the gear is 7.2 cm in diameter. Use a 8x8 cm blank.
For the 16 tooth gear, the gear is 9.2 cm in diameter. Use a 10x10 cm blank.
For the 48 tooth gear, the gear is 26.5 cm in diameter. Use a 27 cm blank.
